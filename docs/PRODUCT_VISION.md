# Product Vision — Smile Transformation Platform

**Goal:** Build the global marketplace for medical tourism, starting with dental tourism in Colombia.

**Core concept:** Patients from the US, Canada, and Europe can discover trusted clinics abroad, compare treatments, and book a medical journey including treatment, travel, and recovery.

**Business model:** Marketplace commission on treatments (15–25%) plus premium concierge services.

---

## Product pillars

1. **Assessment**  
   Patients complete an intelligent assessment describing procedure, budget, and timeline.

2. **AI matching**  
   The platform recommends the best clinic, doctor, and treatment package.

3. **Packages**  
   Curated medical journeys combining treatment, hotel, transportation, and recovery.

4. **Patient portal**  
   Patients can track treatment plans, travel plans, and recovery.

5. **Clinic marketplace**  
   Clinics can manage listings, availability, and patient inquiries.

6. **Payments**  
   Deposits are handled through Stripe checkout.

---

## Long-term vision

Become the **"Airbnb of medical treatments"** connecting international patients with verified clinics worldwide.

---

## Initial focus

**Dental tourism in Colombia.**
